About Us

Our History

We started in 1922 with our bison named Earl and other animals. In April we finally opened our doors to the public.

Then the Great Depression hit. We did everything we could to stay open and keep the animals fed. After WWII, we had plans to moderize our facility.

During the 50s and 60s we had a building boom where we were building new and modern buildings renewing the intrest in the zoo.

During the 70s and 80s Zoos all across the country began coordinating breeding progrms. We became a leader in this endeavour.

Now we are a non-profit organization with an operating agreement from the City of Houston. This partership allows us to work towards out most ambitious projects yet.
