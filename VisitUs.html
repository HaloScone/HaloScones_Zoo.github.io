Here We Are



Parking


We do not have our own parking lot; however, free parking is available in Hermann Park. Remember, on holidays and beautiful days, parking can fill up fast, so make a plan before heading to the Zoo.

Accessibility


The zoo is full of uneven or sloped terrain and may be difficult to navigate for visitors with mobility challenges. We offer wheelchair and electric scooter rentals for those who need them.

Contact Us


If you have any questions or need assistance, please feel free to contact us at 713-533-6500 or visit our information booth at the entrance.
